# Signing In

KeyCase supports multiple authentication methods to get you into the platform quickly and securely.

## Authentication Methods

KeyCase uses **Keycloak** as its identity provider, which supports:

- **Email and password** — Standard credential-based login.
- **Social login** — Sign in with Google, GitHub, or other configured providers.
- **Single Sign-On (SSO)** — Enterprise SSO via SAML or OpenID Connect, when configured by your organization.

## First-Time Login

1. Navigate to your KeyCase instance (e.g., `https://demo.keycase.app`).
2. Click **Sign In** on the landing page.
3. Enter your credentials or choose a social login provider.
4. On first login, you may be prompted to complete your profile and select an organization.

## Selecting an Organization

If you belong to multiple organizations, you'll be asked to choose which one to work in after signing in. You can switch organizations at any time from the sidebar.

## Troubleshooting

- **Forgot password?** — Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen to reset via email.
- **Account locked?** — Contact your organization administrator.
