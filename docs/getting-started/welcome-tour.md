# Welcome Tour

When you first sign in to KeyCase, you'll be offered an interactive **Welcome Tour** that walks you through the platform's main features.

## What the Tour Covers

The welcome tour introduces you to:

1. **Projects** — How to find and select a project to work in.
2. **Keywords** — The building blocks of your automation.
3. **Flows** — How keywords are composed into automation sequences.
4. **Executions** — How to run and monitor your automation.
5. **Navigation** — Key areas of the sidebar and how to move between them.

## Starting the Tour

- The tour launches automatically on your first login.
- To replay it later, open your **User Menu** (top-right) and select **Replay Welcome Tour**.

## Tips

- You can skip individual steps or exit the tour at any time.
- The tour is non-destructive — it doesn't modify any data.
- Each step highlights the relevant part of the interface so you can follow along visually.
